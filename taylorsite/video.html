5 Shot Sequence Video

For this project I had to come up with a story that shows five different shots to explain the story. First, I started by creating my storyboard, then I found someone to film doing what's on my storyboard. I then picked my best shots and used iMovie to edit all of my clips together, to add music, and labels. This project was fairly simple and easy, I think I did a decent job on it. 

Group PSA Video

For this project, I had to work with a group to create a PSA video. For our topic we chose suicide prevention. We started by coming up with our ideas and making a storyboard, then we got the iPad and other equipment and started to film.  After we got done filming we then used iMovie to edit all of our clips together and to add music and a voice over. This project was really easy and simple, the hardest part was actually getting our ideas to actually happen.

Personality Project Screencast

For this project I had to take a personality test to find out my Myers Briggs personality type, then I did research to learn more about it. I got ISTJ for my personality type, so I found information about my personality type and the Myers Briggs test in general and put it all on a PowerPoint, then I filmed a screencast of me talking about it. I think I did a decent job on this project. I got all of the required information. The hardest part was finding all of the information that I needed, and filming it
