Graphics

Photo Composites

F​or this project I had to take pictures of myself and edit them into 10 different photos. First I found all of the pictures that fit each theme on Google, then I added them all to the PowerPoint and fit them to the slide. Then I took pictures of myself where I could fit into each picture. Then I used remove.bg to remove the background of each photo so that I could edit them all into the slides. This project was pretty simple. I think I did an okay job, but I definitely could’ve  done better. One thing that was hard was finding all the photos that fit each theme of the assignment.

Social Media Graphics

For this project I had to make graphics for a social media page. I had to make 3 Instagram posts with quotes. One has to have a solid background, one had to have a pattern as a background, and the other had to have an image as the background. Then we had to make an Instagram post for a real or made up school event. And lastly we had to make a Facebook event cover for the monster truck rally we edited the commercial for in the audio unit. This project was fairly simple, all I had to do was find 3 quotes and add each of them onto one of the posts. and then for the event I decided to do a bake sale, so I made up dates and prices for it and made it look good. Then for the monster truck rally post I got the information from the script then added that onto a background that fit the theme. I think I did a pretty decent job on this project. One of the hardest things was finding good quotes, and making the post match the theme of the quote.

Career and Technical Education (CTE) Month Campaign

For this project I had to make 5 Facebook posts for CTE month. the first had to have a solid background with some type of border, the second had to have a pattern background, the third one had to have a picture in the graphic, the fourth one was anything of my choice, and the last one had to be a post to persuade businesses to let students enrolled in CTE to become an intern at their business. This project was pretty easy. I just found some informative quotes about CTE and edited them over the appropriate background. I think I did a good job with this project. The hardest thing was figuring out what image to do for the 3rd one and what to do for the last one.

Shot Styles and Angles

For this project we had to go out and take pictures with each of the 10 shot styles and angles. Me and my partner got several pictures, then we shared them with each other then we went back to our computer and completed our projects. This project was fairly simple to complete. I first picked the 10 best pictures that fit each category then I added them to Canva and edited the poster to my liking. I think I did a pretty good job with this project. I organized it well and I added some extra stuff to make it look better. The hardest part of this project was getting all of the pictures I needed. Some of the shot styles and angles were easy to get while others weren’t so easy.
